    Ohio State

    Ohio is in the northeastern area of the United States just below Lake Erie. It is one of the most industrious and populated states in the country with almost 12 million citizens. It leads in agricultural and manufacturing products. Dotted across the country-side, you will find rolling hills with forests, large farms, creeks, valleys, and ponds. The state is known as the 'birthplace' of aviation where the first plane was invented by the Wright brothers. Many notable figures of the nations were born and raised in Ohio including 8 US presidents and Niel Armstrong. Ohio is also known as the birthplace of rockin roll
